Here's a time line of University Heart

  • 1964 Dr. Hardy and his team perform the first heart transplant in a human (using a chimpanzee heart).
  • 1997 New Heart Building Built
  • 2010 The pediatric cardiac program hits milestones in congenital heart surgery, expanding capabilities.
  • 2018 UMMC performs its first successful heart-liver transplant, highlighting advancements in complex, multi-organ procedures.
  • 2026 Cyber Attack known as Ransware. Closed all clinics for more than a week.

Who is University Heart

University Heart provides care for heart disease. University Heart is Mississippi's only adult congenital heart program, only heart transplant program, and only LVAD program.

Services They Offer

  • Advanced Heart Failure
  • Cardiac Imaging
